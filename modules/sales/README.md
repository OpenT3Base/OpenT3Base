# Sales & Commerce

The **Sales module** powers all channels—e-commerce, retail, B2B—connecting customers to your products.

## Key Features

- Product catalog and pricing
- Multi-channel sales (web, POS, partners, marketplaces)
- Order, quote, and invoice management
- Promotions and loyalty programs

## Integration

- **Uses** product/inventory data from PLM, MRP.
- **Feeds** sales data to CRM and ERP.
- **Triggers** fulfillment in Logistics.

## Role in OpenT3Base

Sales is where business happens—connecting your offerings with the market.
