# Logistics & Fulfillment

The **Logistics module** manages inventory movement, order fulfillment, shipping, and returns.

## Key Features

- Warehouse and inventory management
- Shipment planning and tracking
- Carrier integration (labels, rates)
- Returns and reverse logistics

## Integration

- **Receives** orders from Sales.
- **Coordinates** with SCM and MES for inventory.
- **Updates** ERP and CRM with delivery status.

## Role in OpenT3Base

Logistics ensures the right product reaches the right customer, on time.
