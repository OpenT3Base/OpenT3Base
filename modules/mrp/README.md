# MRP (Material Requirements Planning)

The **MRP module** transforms product designs into actionable material and production plans.

## Key Features

- Inventory & stock management
- Demand forecasting & supply planning
- Automated purchase requests
- Supplier collaboration (with SCM)
- Shortage alerts & analytics

## Integration

- **Consumes** BOMs and product data from PLM.
- **Works with** SCM for procurement and MES for production scheduling.
- **Updates** ERP with inventory and cost data.

## Role in OpenT3Base

MRP ensures the right materials are available at the right time, optimizing cost and production flow.
