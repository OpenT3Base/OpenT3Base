# SCM (Supply Chain Management)

The **SCM module** connects your business to the global supply chain, managing vendors, contracts, orders, and deliveries.

## Key Features

- Supplier onboarding & rating
- Purchase order and contract management
- Inbound logistics & receiving
- Supplier performance analytics

## Integration

- **Works with** MRP for procurement needs.
- **Integrates with** Logistics for shipment tracking and with ERP for financials.

## Role in OpenT3Base

SCM keeps your supply chain transparent, responsive, and efficient.
