# PLM (Product Lifecycle Management)

The **PLM module** is the digital backbone for managing ideas, product data, engineering changes, and innovation across the full lifecycle—from concept to retirement.

## Key Features

- Idea & requirements management
- Product and variant configuration
- Bill of Materials (BOM) management
- Change management (ECR/ECO)
- Collaboration & workflow tools

## Integration

- **Feeds** product definitions and BOMs to MRP, MES, and ERP.
- **Receives** market/customer feedback from CRM and BI for continuous improvement.

## Role in OpenT3Base

PLM is where value creation starts—integrating engineering, operations, and market needs into a single source of truth.
