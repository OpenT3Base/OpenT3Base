# ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)

The **ERP module** unifies finance, HR, operations, and compliance—serving as the “system of record” for your enterprise.

## Key Features

- Financials (GL, AP, AR)
- HR and payroll
- Asset and facilities management
- Compliance & audit trails

## Integration

- **Aggregates data** from all other modules for reporting and planning.
- **Feeds** financial and HR data to BI.

## Role in OpenT3Base

ERP provides the backbone for business control, planning, and compliance.
