# CRM (Customer Relationship Management)

The **CRM module** centralizes all customer interactions, from marketing to support.

## Key Features

- Customer and account management
- Lead tracking and sales pipeline
- Marketing automation
- Support ticketing and case management

## Integration

- **Integrates with** Sales, ERP, Logistics, and BI for a 360° customer view.

## Role in OpenT3Base

CRM grows relationships, drives loyalty, and fuels growth.
