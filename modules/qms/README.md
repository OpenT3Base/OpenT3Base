# QMS (Quality Management System)

The **QMS module** ensures products and processes meet required quality standards.

## Key Features

- Quality planning and inspection
- Non-conformance management
- Corrective and preventive actions (CAPA)
- Compliance documentation

## Integration

- **Integrates with** MES and ERP for production and compliance data.

## Role in OpenT3Base

QMS reduces defects, assures compliance, and builds customer trust.
